Policy

Thank you for choosing Ellie's Pet Sitting! We want to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for both you and your pets. Please take a moment to review our policies:

Cancellation Policy

Appointments cannot be canceled after 24 hours of confirming the booking. This policy helps us manage our schedule efficiently and ensures that we can provide the best care for all our clients.

Special Needs Dogs

For pets with special needs, we require a form of accidental acceptance to be signed. This form helps us understand the specific requirements of your pet, ensuring that we can provide the necessary care and attention.

Missing Pets

In the rare event that a pet goes missing during our care, we take full responsibility. We will immediately initiate a search and contact local authorities if necessary. Our priority is to locate and safely return your pet as soon as possible.

Health and Safety

We prioritize the health and safety of your pets. If your pet requires medical attention during our care, we will make every effort to contact you and seek veterinary care. By choosing our services, you authorize us to make medical decisions on behalf of your pet in case of an emergency.

Communication

We value open communication. If you have any concerns, special requests, or updates regarding your pet, please feel free to reach out to us. We are here to ensure that you have peace of mind while your pets are in our care.

Feedback

Your feedback is important to us. We appreciate hearing about your experience with Ellie's Pet Sitting, as it helps us continuously improve our services. Feel free to share your thoughts, suggestions, or compliments with our team.
